Simple Omelette Recipe


An easy and quick dish,perfect for any meal.This is classic omellette combines beaten eggs
cooked to perfection,optionally filledwith your choice of cheese,vegetables or meats.


Preparation time

  • Total:Approximately 10 minutes

  • Preparation:5minutes

  • Cooking:5minutes

Ingrindients

  • 2-3 eggs
  • Salt,to taste
  • Pepper,to taste
  • 1tablespoonof butteror oil
  • optional feelings:cheese,diced,vegetables,cooked meats,herbs

Instructions

  • Beat the eggs:with a pinch of salt peeper until they are well mixed.you can add tablespoon for wateror milk for a flufffier texture

  • Heat the pan:Place a non stick frying pan over medium heat and add butter or oil

  • Cook the omelette:Once the butter is melted and the bubling,pour in the eggs.tilt the pan to ensure the eggs evenly coat in the surface

  • Add feelings(optional):when the eggs begin to set at the edges but are still slightly runny in the middle,sprinkel your chosen feelings over on half of the omelette

  • Fold and serve:Asthe omelette continues to cookcarefully lift one edgeand fold it overthe feelings.Let it cook for another minute,then slide it onto aplate

  • EnjoyServe hot withadditional saltand pepper if needed
